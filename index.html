FAQ

Common questions

What exactly is in the free first audit? ↓
The free audit covers your brand across 5 buyer-intent prompts queried against at least two AI tiers (bulk + reason). You get a visibility score, which models mentioned you, top competitors, and a short fix list. It's the same format as a paid report, just a one-time snapshot.
Which AI models do you query? ↓
Currently: ChatGPT/GPT-4o, Gemini, Perplexity, Grok, and Claude, queried through a multi-LLM orchestration layer. Coverage expands as new flagship models are released. We document exactly which models ran in every report.
How is the 0–100 score calculated? ↓
The score weights three factors: mention rate (50 pts, how often you appear across all queries), rank quality (30 pts, whether you appear as #1, #2, etc.), and sentiment (20 pts, positive vs. neutral vs. negative language about your brand). Full methodology is in the report.
Why does tracking change over time matter? ↓
LLMs are retrained and updated frequently. A brand that scores 80 today can drop to 40 after a model update, because the new training data favored competitors. The only way to catch this is continuous monitoring, not a one-time snapshot.
Can agencies resell this? ↓
Yes. The Agency tier ($499/mo) includes white-label PDF reports with your logo. You can sell them as a standalone monthly GEO audit service at your own margin. Up to 10 brands per account.
What can I actually do to improve my score? ↓
Each report includes a prioritized action list. Common high-impact fixes: building brand entity signals (Wikipedia, Wikidata, Google Knowledge Panel), generating high-quality topical content that LLMs are likely trained on, getting cited in authoritative roundups, and ensuring your brand name is unambiguous. We tailor recommendations to your specific gap.
